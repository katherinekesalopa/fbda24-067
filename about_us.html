About Viva Travels

At Viva Travels, we are passionately committed to crafting unforgettable African adventures that leave lasting memories. Established in 2018, our mission is to make travel accessible, seamless, and stress-free by offering our innovative no-upfront-payment booking system, designed to provide flexibility and peace of mind. We take pride in curating exceptional experiences that highlight the breathtaking diversity of Africa’s landscapes, from the sweeping savannas of the Serengeti teeming with wildlife to the vibrant cultural tapestry of Cape Town and the awe-inspiring majesty of Victoria Falls. Our expertly tailored itineraries immerse you in the rich traditions, thrilling activities, and unparalleled natural beauty of the continent, ensuring every moment of your journey is extraordinary.

Our Mission

At Viva Travels, our goal is to eliminate financial obstacles to exploration by providing an innovative no-upfront-payment system, empowering travelers from all walks of life to experience the breathtaking wonders of Africa without the burden of immediate costs. Our dedicated team is wholeheartedly committed to designing highly personalized journeys that showcase the continent’s extraordinary beauty, from its sprawling savannas and majestic wildlife to its vibrant cultures and rich historical heritage. Whether you’re an adventure seeker craving thrilling escapades or a family longing for meaningful shared experiences, we meticulously craft each itinerary to create cherished, lifelong memories that celebrate the unparalleled diversity and splendor of Africa.
